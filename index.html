About

Hardware meets firmware

I am an EPFL Robotics Master's student with a minor in Computer Science. I am naturally curious about complex systems and enjoy tinkering with the intersection of hardware and firmware to understand how they function.

My professional experience includes a role as a Test System Intern at ONWARD Medical. I am currently an Electronics Engineering Intern at Melexis, where I integrate magnetic sensors into edge-AI capable wireless devices.

On campus, I lead a team of 12 for the EPFL AI Team, overseeing the design and development of an autonomous robot dog. I am also an electronics engineer for Xplore, contributing to the avionics and sensor systems of a Mars rover for the European Rover Challenge.

In my free time, I enjoy woodworking and hiking.
